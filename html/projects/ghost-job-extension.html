Ghost Job Extension

Type Browser Extension
Tagline Spot suspicious listings while you browse job boards

Ghost Job Extension brings ghost-job detection directly into your browsing workflow. Instead of copying job descriptions into a separate tool, the extension analyzes listing content in-page and flags risk signals like stale repost patterns, vague requirement language, and pipeline-style recruiting copy. Each score includes transparent reasoning so you can quickly decide whether a listing is worth applying to.

Highlights

  • In-page analysis for faster job triage without context switching.
  • Signal-based scoring across stale, vague, and pipeline dimensions.
  • Plain-language risk explanations to keep decisions transparent.
